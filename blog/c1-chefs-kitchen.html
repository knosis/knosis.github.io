Just Enough Cooks in the Kitchen

This is for beginners

May 31st, 2015

The cohort and Google+ are a tremendous support group which helps a lot when you are taking on a new adventure. This is exactly the kind of environment I hoped. People are nervous but they seem to be working through their anxiety. Once we have moved the phase 1, we will really see how the group will come together.

I am not nervous, but that does not mean I do not anticipate difficulty. My current job has a very hectic busy season and I've learned that anxiety doesn't help. Planning and working through issues is the best solution. Progress is one of the best anxiety relievers for me.

I've learned that asking for help is effective. People in this cohort are quick to respond.

In the Weeds

This is for beginners

June 7, 2015

I didn't grew up with a strong sense of faith, but I've always been optimistic. It's not the best substitute, but it helps. My stubbornness has bestowed me with a growth mindset. It's a good way for me to make the best of a weakness.

There is a good amount of wisdom in self knowledge. I retook an exam on my thinking style. It's probably the reason I learn so much from paired programming. It's more personal, more visual, more hands-on and allows for brainstorming. The scheduling portion of it goes against my carefree lifestyle, but for the most part it's been a large plus. I will try to take advantage of it in the future to help tackle some of the more difficult assignments. Two heads are better than one.
